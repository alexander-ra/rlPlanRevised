# Email Drafting Instructions for PhD Supervisors

When asked to draft an email to the PhD supervisors (Prof. Vasilev and Assoc. Prof. Rusev), ALWAYS follow these guidelines:

## 1. Style and Tone
- **Language:** Bulgarian.
- **Tone:** Professional, academic, concise, and highly direct. Avoid corporate "fluff" or overly long introductions.
- **Subject Line:** Use descriptive and formal subjects like "Относно: Докторантура – Александър Андреев: [Тема на мейла]".

## 2. Core Content Rules
- **Generalization over Domain:** Always emphasize that the research focus is on general "multi-agent imperfect-information environments" (мултиагентни среди с непълна информация), applicable to fraud detection/cybersecurity. Strictly avoid framing the PhD as "poker-focused".
- **Contributions:** When discussing goals, clearly format the 3 core contributions as bullet points (Behavioral Adaptation, Safe Exploitation for >2 players, Evaluation Methodology).
- **Time/Scope Estimations:** When discussing timelines, emphasize that the plan includes precise time estimations per phase, with the goal of incrementally building working drafts of dissertation chapters (e.g., Chapter 1).
- **Attachments / Document References:** Do NOT state that technical documents (like the architecture plan) are attached unless explicitly told to do so. Frame the email as a status update that promises specific deliverables (e.g., "I will send the text by the end of the week").

## 3. General Context
- **Addressing:** "Уважаеми проф. Василев, доц. Русев,"
- **Sign-off:** "Поздрави, Александър Андреев"
