# The 5-Year MARL Career Roadmap (Bulgaria Strategy)

**Profile:** 10 YOE Senior Front-End/UX Engineer transitioning via a PhD in Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) with a focus on exploitation.
**Location:** Sofia, Bulgaria (Optimizing for geo-arbitrage and family).
**Financial Target:** €120k Net / ~€140k+ Gross B2B.

---

## 🎯 The Core Strategy
Stop competing in the commoditized front-end market. Use the PhD to master the underlying math and infrastructure of autonomous systems. Use the current online poker job as a live, adversarial laboratory to build a production-grade portfolio. Transition into high-value, remote B2B consulting.

---

## 🏆 The High-Value Targets (The "Do This" List)

### 1. B2B MARL Solutions Architect / Principal AI Engineer (The Ultimate Goal)
* **The Play:** Transition from UI/UX into a specialized independent contractor. Architect complex multi-agent simulations (fintech, ride-sharing, cybersecurity) and hand off the deployed pipeline to the client's internal engineers.
* **Why it Wins:** Bypasses local Bulgarian salaries. You maintain ultimate geographic freedom while easily clearing your target.
* **The Moat:** You know the complex math (PhD) *and* how to deploy scalable production software (10 YOE). You are a unicorn.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** * **Hourly:** $100 - $160 / hour.
  * **Annual Gross:** €140,000 - €220,000 (depending on billable hours and client US/EU location).

### 2. iGaming/Fintech Fraud & Risk Consultant (The Natural Evolution)
* **The Play:** You leverage your current poker employer. You use MARL to build bot-detection and economic stress-testing systems. You then package that exact skillset and consult for massive iGaming platforms or payment processors.
* **Why it Wins:** You are already in the laboratory. Companies will pay massive retainers to protect their liquidity from sophisticated AI rings.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** * **Project-Based:** €30,000 - €60,000 per 2-to-3-month audit/build engagement.
  * **Retainers:** €3,000 - €5,000 / month per client just for ongoing model monitoring. 
  * **Annual Gross Target:** €150,000 - €250,000+ (doing 3-4 projects a year plus retainers).

### 3. Web3 / DeFi "MEV" Researcher (The High-Paying Niche)
* **The Play:** Apply your PhD in MARL *exploitation* to build bots that find mathematical inefficiencies in decentralized finance (DeFi) smart contracts.
* **Why it Wins:** The crypto/DeFi sector routinely pays top-tier US salaries to fully remote workers in Eastern Europe. It is the perfect 1:1 application of adversarial game theory.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** * **Base Salary (Remote DAO/Fund):** $150,000 - $250,000 USD base.
  * **Upside:** Often includes profit-sharing or token grants, pushing Total Comp well past $300k.

### 4. AI Tooling & MLOps UX Architect (The Perfect Bridge)
* **The Play:** Build the bespoke, high-performance web dashboards where human experts review complex MARL environments (RLHF tooling) and XAI (Explainable AI) observability platforms.
* **Why it Wins:** It leverages your entire 10 YOE in UX, but elevates you out of the "commodity web dev" tier to build control panels for autonomous systems.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** * **Hourly:** $80 - $130 / hour.
  * **Annual Gross:** €120,000 - €160,000.

## 🚫 The Traps (The "Avoid This" List)

### 6. Technical Director / VP of Engineering (The "Golden Handcuffs" Trap)
* **The Play:** Running the whole engineering department using your broad knowledge stack.
* **Why it Loses:** Wastes the hardest parts of your brain on PTO approvals and budget fights.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** €80,000 - €120,000 Gross (Benchmarked to EU/Bulgarian local market rates, making it harder to hit your €120k *net* goal than B2B consulting, plus far higher stress).

### 7. Pure ML Researcher at TradFi Hedge Funds or FAANG (The Lifestyle Killer)
* **The Play:** Competing for pure research roles at places like Citadel or DeepMind.
* **Why it’s a Dealbreaker:** Zero geographic freedom. They will demand relocation to London, NY, or SF, destroying your family lifestyle goals.
* **💰 Estimated Pay:** $350,000 - $800,000+ Total Comp. (Looks amazing until you factor in London/NYC taxes, $5,000/month rent, and the fact that you can't live in Bulgaria).

## 🚀 The 5-Year Execution Plan

1.  **Survive the Math & Infrastructure:** Push through the PhD. Focus heavily on low-level infrastructure (C++, Rust, Ray, distributed computing) alongside the game theory (CFR).
2.  **Exploit the Poker Lab:** Pitch your Technical Director on an internal project. Build a bot-detection or game-economy MARL agent using real company hand-history data. Deploy it to production.
3.  **Shed the "Front-End" Label:** Rebrand entirely as an AI/Systems Architect. Use your UX skills as a secondary superpower, not your primary identity.
